Finest Lasagna Recipe

How to make top tier Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and 2 cloves of garlic.
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes.
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Homemade lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzaralla, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Step-By-Step Process

Brief overview of what to expect:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

Layer the lasagna in the following order:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.

Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.

Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
